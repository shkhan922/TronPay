Warning

The Bronwyn Protocol is a highly experimental protocol. We encourage all individuals to read up on the protocol before engaging with the presale. By participating in the presale, the user will have the earliest possible access to the stable growth fund.

Presale Rules

  1. do not send ethereum from any exchange accounts (binance, coinbase, etc.). do not send funds from any wallet where you do not have the private keys. we suggest using a browser wallet such as metamask or trustwallet
  2. all individuals who partake in the presale and hold the Bronsale token (BRSL) will be granted governance rights and will have access to exclusive high-capital Treasury Rebalance events
  3. all users are encouraged to contribute as much as they like (above .5 eth) and will be awarded BRSL tokens in proportion to how much they contribute
  4. the funds will be used to mint 15,000 BRON tokens in total: 2,000 of which will be awarded to airdrop winners. the remaining 13,000 BRON tokens will be distributed amongst the wallets that contributed funds to the presale
  5. the airdrop awardees will not receive BRSL unless they contribute to the presale.
  6. aside from the BRSL token, users who contributed funds for the presale will also be awarded the BRON token
  7. the presale will officially be closed on december 22th 00:00 UTC
